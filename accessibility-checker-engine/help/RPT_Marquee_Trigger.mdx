## Who does this affect?

* People with attention deficit disorders
* People with reading disabilities
* People with intellectual disabilities
* People with low vision

## Why is this important?

Content that moves or auto-updates can be a barrier and a severe distraction to anyone who has trouble reading stationary text quickly as well as anyone who has trouble tracking moving objects. The `<marquee>` element is obsolete and should not be used.

## Scrolling content found that uses the obsolete `<marquee>` element
The `<marquee>` element is obsolete and should not be used

[IBM 2.2.2 Pause, Stop, Hide](http://www.ibm.com/able/guidelines/ci162/pause_stop_hide.html)

<div id="locSnippet"></div>

## What to do

* If it isn't absolutely necessary, don't use this effect;
* OR, use other options such as CSS or scripting to create the same effect, and include an option to turn off the scrolling.
